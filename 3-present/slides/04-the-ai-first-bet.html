Differentiating thesis

AI-first means the system acts inside the workflow — not beside it in a chat panel

On the floor: embedded directives

Next pick, carton call, and fix action live in the handheld — not a copilot thread off to the side.

At ship: enforced compliance

Non-compliant shipments hard-block at label print and ship scan — the system stops the action, not just the dashboard.

Everywhere: operator override with audit trail

Every directive has a visible human exit — audited, never silent; overrides feed master data hygiene.

Enforcement at scan time beats chat summary Honest framing: 1 AI-native capability + 2 AI-enhanced optimizations in Phase 1
