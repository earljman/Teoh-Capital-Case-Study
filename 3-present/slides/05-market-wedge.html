Ideal customer profile assumption

We're building for the missing middle: outgrown small and medium business (SMB) tools, priced out of enterprise AI

Digitally native mid-market omnichannel brands and independent e-commerce 3PLs. They've hit the headcount ceiling — order volume grows, but lines-per-hour flatlines — and can't absorb a $50k+ implementation or a nine-month enterprise rollout.

  • SMB warehouse management tops out around 10k orders/month; operators brute-force with seasonal labor
  • Enterprise machine learning (ML) sits behind $250k+ TCO and dedicated IT — inaccessible at this scale
  • $3k–8k/month budget is ready if time-to-value is clear — 4–8 week sales cycles, not 12-month RFPs
