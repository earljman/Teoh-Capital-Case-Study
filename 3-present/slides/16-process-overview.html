Operating model

Research -> Planning -> Development -> Present

AI accelerated production in every phase; human checkpoints controlled decision quality and final direction.

01 · Research

Use AI research workflows to map market and operator context quickly

  • AI output: competitor landscape, market sizing, journey and stakeholder drafts
  • Human gate: selected only evidence that changed scope or ICP assumptions
02 · Planning

Convert research drafts into hard product decisions

  • AI output: problem framing options, PRD slices, and checkpoint drafts
  • Human gate: locked the differentiating bet, top-3 scope, and roadmap order
03 · Development

Ship implementation-grade screens and a live demo path

  • AI output: accelerated UI iteration, copy variants, and implementation scaffolding
  • Human gate: verified states, interaction clarity, and narrative consistency
04 · Present

Package evidence into a reviewer-ready story and appendix

  • AI output: draft tightening, structure checks, and rapid appendix assembly
  • Human gate: final edit for credibility, coherence, and interview defensibility
Evidence trail: 0-research/ -> 1-plan/ -> 2-build/demo/ -> 3-present/ AI accelerated the workflow; judgment remained human-owned.
