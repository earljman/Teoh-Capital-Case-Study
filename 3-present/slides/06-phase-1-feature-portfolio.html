01 · AI-native

Retail shipping rules

Retailers fine you 2–5% of wholesale sales for label and packing mistakes. AI turns PDF routing guides into checkable rules — a manager approves them, then the system won't let a bad shipment leave.

02 · AI-enhanced

Smarter pick routes

Pickers spend 40–60% of the shift walking between shelves. The system picks a shorter route and finds a new one in seconds when a bin is empty. Supervisors can override a whole batch.

03 · AI-enhanced

Right-size every box

About 20% of freight cost is shipping air in boxes that are too big. Packers get one recommended box and a packing diagram. If they choose differently, they say why — so bad product measurements get fixed.
