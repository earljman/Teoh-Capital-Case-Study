Phase 1 Signal 08 / 09
Candidate top 3 · from gaps + journey

Three bets that occupy the white space.

01 · Orchestration

AI Task Orchestration

Dynamic prioritization, pick-path optimization, and task interleaving during release, run, and recovery — continuous queues vs static waves.

02 · Exceptions

AI Exception Copilot

Ranked exception inbox with recommended actions, one-tap assign, and always-on override — short picks, ASN mismatch, inventory drift.

03 · Command

AI Ops Command Center

Shift brief, live KPI risk, recovery playbooks, and accuracy signals for supervisors — decisions, not dashboards.
